New Istanbul Airport (IST)

Atatürk Airport has completed its life after serving Istanbul for years. It left its place to Istanbul Airport, which was newly built and one of the largest airports in the world. This is the flight code (IST) for the new airport.

New Istanbul Airport started its lifetime in 6th of April 2019.

It is Istanbul’s main airport with international flights. It is located in Arnavutköy region, which is northwest of Istanbul.

Turkey’s national air carrier Turkish Airlines, this airport is the main hub.

When all stages are completed, it will be able to serve 200 million passengers a year.

In addition, the entire airport and its surroundings:

It is guarded by 3500 security personnel, 750 immigration officers and 1850 police officers.

  • There are 9000 cameras in the terminal building.
  • CCTV camera every 60 meters
  • Pan-Tilt-Zoom Camera every 360 meters
  • There are thermal and optical cameras every 720 meters.
  • Istanbul Airport Coronavirus Security Measures

Istanbul Airport Security Measurements for Coronavirus

Istanbul Airport has taken serious measures due to Coronavirus.

  • All passengers must wear masks both in the terminal building and in the area.
  • Contactless security measures were taken in security control areas.
  • Body temperatures of all passengers are controlled by thermal cameras.
  • All passenger baggage passing through X-Ray is sterilized with ultraviolet.
  • Hand sterilization fluids are available in all areas open to passengers.
  • Terminal buildings are sterilized by autonomous robots and UV light.
  • Social distance is constantly reminded with informative signs.
  • 24/7 fresh air circulation is provided to the terminal.
  • Passenger buses in the area are disinfected after each use.
  • It established a test center with a 5000 m ^ 2 area and a capacity of 10,000 tests per day.
  • PCR, Antibody and Antigen tests are performed 24/7 at Istanbul airport. You can pay by credit card or cash for these tests, where you can get results quickly.
  • You can quickly get the results of PCR, Antibody and Antigen tests performed in these centers.
Is Istanbul Airport Safe to Travel?

Comparing the global precautions , It can be said that Istanbul airport is one of the most safe airports in the world considering the above pre-cautions.

Where is Istanbul Airport Corona-virus Test Center?

The test center is located at the exit gate number 14 “Arriving Passenger”.

How to make Coronavirus test in Istanbul Airport

If you want to be a Covid-19 PCR test, you can follow the steps below.
* Click the “I want to test” button to get a sequence number.
* Make the test payment by credit card or cash.
* Get your bar-code.
* Go to the test taking section and give your test sample.
* If you want to know the result of your test immediately, press the relevant button.

Istanbul Airport Ground Transportation

New Istanbul Airport is located roughly 40 km away from the city center, Taksim. The map will help you to visualize location.

There are currently certain options for transportation from Istanbul airport.

Subway Transportation

Currently the subway connection is not implemented yet.
The M11 Gayrettepe – Istanbul Airport line is being constructed. It is planning that M11 Metro Line will be connected to Istanbul Airport in 2022.

Uber

Uber is currently banned by the government in order to showing position on behalf of the taxi drivers before the general elections. However, a new judicial decision remove the blockage. Next days, we will see if UBER is an option or not.

We hope that the block on UBER will be removed.

Taxi

Taxi will costs you around $ 30 – $ 35 and it takes 35-45mins. Those are estimations considering the low traffic and the shortest path. However, our experiences with taxis as a passenger of local citizens is negative. Except the honest drivers, It is highly probable that a taxi driver can manipulate the road.

Of course these are personal experiences. However, it is very common.

Havaist Shuttle Bus

Havaist is a shuttle service that costs you around 30 TL. You can only leave the shuttle bus on pre-defined destinations. It has more than 30 pre-defined destinations. It is one of the most economical transportation ways from Istanbul Airport.

If you download the mobile apps, you can learn the schedule. Moreover, you can pay the ticket on the app.

Havaist iOS App on App Store

Municipality Bus

The official public bus service of Istanbul is IETT. It is the most cheapest transfer option. If you are planning to make Istanbul travel economical, you should get an Istanbul Card. It is a contact-less card that you can deposit money in more than 2000 points. It is valid for nearly all public transportation cases. You can use it with tram, subway, bus, ferry etc.

Private Transfer

Another solution is a private transfer service. It is basically a pre-booked service you made before your flight. Despite the taxi drivers, private transfer company or the service provider know your destination. Therefore it is less open to surprises. It is a professional service that costs you between $ 40 – $ 200. Price fluctuation is becuase of the quality of the service you demand.
