Best Places to Eat in Istanbul City Center

Best Places to Eat in Beyoglu

A Must Try Restaurant: FICCIN

FICCIN is one of the legendary restaurants of Beyoğlu Kallavi Street. Run by two distinguished sisters, this restaurant is one of the favorite restaurants of those working in the Beyoğlu region. It can be preferred for breakfast, lunch and dinner. They have a wide range of menus including the rich flavors of Circassian cuisine.

Fıccın – Çerkes ve Anadolu Mutfağı – Circassian&Anatolian Cuisine

A Specific Food You Must Try: Lades Restaurant

Menemen is basically a local food which prepared with eggs scrambled with tomatoes and green pepper. It looks like a simple food. However, if you try it on Lades Restaurant, you’ll be amazed. It has been rated the Best Menemen Maker by newspapers for years.

You should definitely eat menemen in this unpretentious restaurant, which is a place you will not notice if you pass by.

Katip Mustafa Çelebi Mahallesi, İstiklal Caddesi, Sadri Alışık Sokak, No 14/A, Beyoğlu, İstanbul

Nizam Pide

Nizam Pide (Pita) is a restaurant that serves local Turkish and Anatolian cuisine. Their milk desserts are just as delicious as their kebabs. You can find this restaurant around Galatasaray.

www.nizampide.com

Kalyoncu Kulluk Cd. No: 13

Best Liver Maker: Canım Ciğerim

Canım Cigerim is a tiny place that has short chairs with traditional serving. It is known as the best liver maker in the Turkey. If you are not vegan or vegetarian, you should definitely visit and taste their food.

Best Local Fast Food in Istanbul: Kızılkayalar

You’ll see many local fast-food company near Taksim square. Despite all of them looks similar, Kizilkayalar is a distinguished one.The place is open 24/7.

Address: Sıraselviler Caddesi NO:2L, 34433
